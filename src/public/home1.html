About us

Founder

>

CoFounder

VindhyaMotors, a startup that has been in operation since 2005, embarked on its journey from the historic city of Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh. In 2021, we further solidified our presence in the region by establishing another center in the bustling city of Varanasi. With a footprint now in two major cities of Uttar Pradesh, VindhyaMotors has become a cornerstone in the automotive service industry. We pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive range of services to our customers, including car wash, car polish, detailing, painting, accident handling, insurance assistance, parts replacement, and more. With a dedicated team and unwavering commitment to quality, we have garnered the trust of over 250 satisfied customers each month across both our centers. As we look to the future, our vision extends beyond regional boundaries; we are on the cusp of expanding our centers across India, aiming to provide our exceptional services to even more customers nationwide.
